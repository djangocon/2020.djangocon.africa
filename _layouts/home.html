about

DjangoCon Africa is a five-day international conference for the community by the community about the Django web framework.

DjangoCon Africa will be held in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia for the first time ever, made up of Django practitioners from all levels. We welcome people from all over the world. Our conference seeks to educate and develop new skills, best practices and ideas for the benefit of attendees, developers, speakers and everyone in our global Django Community, not least those watching the talks online.

Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a country in North East of Africa, commonly known as the Horn of Africa. It is a country with a rich history and many historical places to visit.
